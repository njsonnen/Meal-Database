Tacos

- Ingredients (aldi/jacks)
1-3 pounds of ground chuck(depending on leftovers)
lettuce			tomatos
shredded cheese		ortega
salsa			tortilla shells
ground cumin		black pepper
chili powder		seasoned salt
cayenne pepper		sour cream

1. place meat in pan on high and start breaking it up
2. cook until browned and drain out the meat with some water
3. put all the seasoning on with the heat on low and mix
4. dice up tomatoes and lettuce and thats basically it
