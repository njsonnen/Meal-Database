Salmon and asparagus

-Ingredients (aldi)
1. 1 or 2 packages of salmon
2. Asparagus
3. Salt
4. Pepper
5. Butter

-Instructions:
1. place fish in pan on hot
2. get another pan on hot and throw on butter with asparagus
3. put salt and pepper in with asparagus and flip the fish
4. Make sure the fish is cooked all the way through
